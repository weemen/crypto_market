Sell bitcoins to LiteBit

You sell Bitcoins and other cryptocurrencies to LiteBit.eu in exchange for euro or credits. We will execute payments on 11.30 and 16.30

Selling bitcoins, how does it work?

With every new sell order we create a new and unique deposit address to which you need to send the currency. When you do not make the payment within the 15 minute mark we recalculate the order for recent rates.

Tips

  1. We execute payments every workday at 11.30 and 16.30. We do not execute payments during weekends or holidays.
  2. We strongly advice to send your coins with miners fee to make sure the coins are received by LiteBit within the 15 minutes time limit.
